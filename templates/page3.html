                </div>

Add both d-flex and justify-content-end to justify the button to the right side

                <div class="col-6 border border-primary d-flex justify-content-end my-2">
                    <button class="btn btn-primary">button</button>
                </div>

Add both d-flex and justify-content-end to justify the button to the right side - then add w-50 to fill 50% of the col

                <div class="col-6 border border-primary d-flex justify-content-end my-2">
                    <button class="btn btn-primary w-50">button</button>
                </div>

Add both d-flex and justify-content-center to justify the button in the middle - then add w-50 to fill 50% of the col

                <div class="col-6 border border-primary d-flex justify-content-center my-2">
                    <button class="btn btn-primary w-50">button</button>
                </div>

Add justify-content-start to justify the button at the start - then add w-50 to fill 50% of the col

                <div class="col-6 border border-primary justify-content-start my-2">
                    <button class="btn btn-primary w-50">button</button>
                </div>

Or leave out the justify-content-start since that's the default

                <div class="col-6 border border-primary my-2">
